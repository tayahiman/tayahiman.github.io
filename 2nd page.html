Top 10 Most Watched Christmas Movies During the Holidays

Christmas movies are a fan favorite in many households, as they provide loving, sometimes whimsical stories of the joys that Christmastime brings.

According to a public poll from The Irish Post,these movies are listed in order as the top 10 favorite Christmas movies to watch during the holidays. Pop some popcorn, warm up some hot cocoa and tune in to these fan favorites!

  • Home Alone:24%
  • Elf:22%
  • The Grinch:18%
  • Love Actually:18%
  • Its a Wonderful Life:17%
  • Miracle on 34th Street:16%
  • The Snowman:13%
  • The Muppet Christmas Carol:12%
  • White Christmas:10%
  • The Santa Clause:8%
